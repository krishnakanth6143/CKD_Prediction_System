About Kidney Stone Detection

What are kidney stones?

Kidney stones are hard deposits made of minerals and salts that form inside your kidneys. They can affect any part of your urinary tract from your kidneys to your bladder. Often, stones form when the urine becomes concentrated, allowing minerals to crystallize and stick together.

How our detection works

Our AI model analyzes medical images such as CT scans or ultrasounds to identify potential kidney stones. The model has been trained on thousands of images to accurately detect stones of various sizes and compositions.

What to expect

Upload a clear image of your CT scan or ultrasound. The AI will analyze the image and provide a preliminary assessment. Please note that this tool is for educational purposes only and should not replace professional medical advice.

Next steps

If kidney stones are detected, consult with a healthcare provider for proper diagnosis and treatment options. Early detection and treatment can prevent complications.
