About Kidney Health

Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD), kidney stones, and kidney tumors are significant health concerns affecting millions. CKD involves gradual kidney function loss, often linked to diabetes and hypertension, while kidney stones and tumors can cause pain and complications.

Our tools enable early detection: CKD prediction uses clinical parameters, while kidney stone and tumor detection analyze medical images (e.g., CT scans). Early intervention can improve outcomes for all conditions.

Monitor your kidney health with regular check-ups, especially if you have risk factors like family history or lifestyle issues.
